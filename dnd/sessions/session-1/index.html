Session 1

Summary

Prince Luca had the Royal Ballroom set up as a super cool awesome casino night. Luca, Connie, and Ryder all did a good ole fashioned lick n’ sniff on some Pepsi. The players eventually made their way over to the main roulette table and watched as Luca gambled an obscene amount of money and lost it. Joag has the Urish Mafia start an investigation on David. Connie, Ryder, and Balthier check out the library and adventurers’ guild to get information on artifacts, geography, history, etc. Zulden tells the temple he’s going off on a pilgrim and there was much rejoicing.
