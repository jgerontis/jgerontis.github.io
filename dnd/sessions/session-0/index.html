Lore Summary

The New World was discovered ~1000 years ago by the Utalian explorer Amerigo Vespucci. Luckily, there were absolutely no sentient races already living there. He went back to Utaly in the Old World, where news of his travels spread very quickly. The various races of the old world all hastily sent out colony ships to claim vast swathes of this new and exciting land.

The various Human nations built bustling, independent city-states of commerce and innovation along the East Coast.

The Dwarf kingdoms settled in the Bouldery mountain range, quickly building deep mines and massive keeps of stone.

Running from persecution, several colonies of Tieflings just went up and over the North pole, and built safe havens where all could live without fear of prejudice.

The Elves, who already lived on the eastern side of the Old World, were the first to cross the Spacific Ocean, and build majestic settlements across the Western Coast.

In an attempt to not spread the warring ways of their homelands to this majestic new land, diplomats from all the lands gathered and signed the treaty of Mount Saint Van Halen’s - ushering in a millennia of peace and prosperity.
