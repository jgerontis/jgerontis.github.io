Zulden

  • Race: Aasimar
  • Title: Novice Cleric (Warlock)
  • Affiliation: Temple of Mishikal
  • Location: New Yark
  • First Seen: Session 0
  • Status: Alive
  • Age: 40

Description

6’1, fair pale skin, blonde hair and pale yellow eyes. Has a slight halo when in the sunlight

Personality

Extroverted and caring. Extremely devoted to Mishikal, but not outwardly pushy about it. Outwardly friendly towards most people.

Background

Comes from the temple of Mishikal, and was raised there. Spends his time healing the people of New Yark.

Relationships

Buddies with Prince Luca through his work at the temple. No normal parental structure at the temple, as we all live for each other and our Goddess. No siblings. No lovers. No real enemies, besides people who are “evil” and want to cause pain and suffering on the people of New Yark.
